About Me

I am a Senior Machine Learning Engineer at CARIAD SE (Volkswagen Group), working within the World Models & Semantics team. My current focus is on developing models and pipelines for ground truth generation needed for various functions in the autonomous driving stack.

Simultaneously, I am pursuing my PhD at the Technical University of Berlin in the Computer Vision & Remote Sensing Lab. My research sits at the intersection of self-supervised monocular depth estimation, 3D scene reconstruction, and metric-scale geometry for vehicular perception.

I have published at WACV, 3DV, and CVPR and have served as a reviewer for 3DV and CVPR.
