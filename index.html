Senior ML Engineer at CARIAD SE  ·  PhD Candidate, TU Berlin

I work in the World Models & Semantics team at CARIAD SE (Volkswagen Group), developing models and pipelines for ground truth generation in autonomous driving. My PhD research at TU Berlin's Computer Vision & Remote Sensing Lab focuses on self-supervised monocular depth estimation and metric-scale 3D perception. I have published at WACV, 3DV, and CVPR, and served as a reviewer for 3DV and CVPR.
