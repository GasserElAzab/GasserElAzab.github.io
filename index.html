Gasser Elazab

Senior ML Engineer at CARIAD SE  ·  PhD Candidate, TU Berlin

I am a Senior Machine Learning Engineer at CARIAD SE (Volkswagen Group) within the World Models & Semantics team, where I develop models and pipelines for lane detection and ground truth processing for the autonomous driving stack. I hold a PhD from TU Berlin's Computer Vision & Remote Sensing Lab, specializing in 3D road surface reconstruction from monocular images. My academic background includes a dual MSc from KTH Royal Institute of Technology and TU Berlin. I have published at CVPR, WACV, and 3DV, and regularly serve as a reviewer for CVPR and 3DV.
